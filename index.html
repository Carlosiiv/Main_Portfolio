About Me

My Journey!

Hello and Welcome! As mentioned above, I am Carlos Michel. I am a Full Stack Developer ready and able to apply the many skills that I have acquired. I have a background in Transportation, Marketing, and Military Operations. I am also a Veteran of the U.S Air Force.

I chose to make the transition to Web Development and Programming in 2019. I reached a point in my career where i was not being challenged, which led me on a path that sparked my interest in programming. I have always had a passion for computers due to having built many personal computers in the past. I ended up becoming very intrigued by the broad possibilities of building webpages and applications utilizing basic technologies. I began to look for more organized forms of learning, and landed on Khan Academy & FreeCodeCamp. After a few months of exhausting those assets, I was left with a hunger for more, and found Coding Dojo. I several months expanding on the base knowledge I had built on my own.
Being immersed in a programming environment has led me to new heights in my programming journey. Unfortunately due to Covid-19 the course was moved to a remote environment. I was quickly able to adapt and become a more self sufficient coder and student. While at Coding Dojo I have expanded on my Python skill set by adding Django and Flask Frameworks. As well as SQLite Database application. I have also learned C#(ASP.Net) with Entity Framework. As well as utilizing MySql for the database. Through this expansion of information at my disposal i was able to easily transition to writing scripts for unity based games, and creating data driven applications through NumPy.

I am currently in the process of completing my final stack(MERN). I am ready to take on complex projects, build on my current knowledge base, and thrive in any environment.
